How do plants reorganize chromatin in response to changing environments?

Our lab studies how environmental signals reshape chromatin architecture, epigenetic states, and genome organization to regulate plant growth and adaptation.

Research Themes

Environmental Chromatin Remodeling

We study how light and temperature trigger changes in chromatin accessibility and histone modifications.

3D Genome Architecture

Investigating how chromatin loops and higher-order organization contribute to environmental adaptation.

Climate Resilience

Translating mechanistic discoveries to crop species relevant to food security and climate change.
